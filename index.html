product brief
IIM

"Fresh4Sure is not just a product but a business model that can institutionalize the complete fruit supply chain process."

 NIT

"Fresh4Sure is a good initiative for the society."

Every fruit have a natural layering in the form of wax that helps the fruits from going bad, but as the time progresses, the fruit loses that layer.
Fresh4Sure imitates that natural layering after extracting a solution from waste fruit extracts which creates a natural and edible layer that helps in increasing the shelf life of the produce by 2-3 times.
In this way, we are using the fruit waste to reduce the fruit wastage.

  • increases shelf life
  • edible coating on fruits
  • tasteless
  • non-allergic
  • odorless
  • colorless
  • eco-friendly

Every fruit have a natural layering in the form of wax that helps the fruits from going bad, but as the time progresses, the fruit loses that layer.
Fresh4Sure imitates that natural layering after extracting a solution from waste fruit extracts which creates a natural and edible layer that helps in increasing the shelf life of the produce by 2-3 times. In this way, we are using the fruit waste to reduce the fruit wastage.

First, the waste fruit extracts would be procured from mainly the fruit juice producing companies and from that the lipid extraction process would be carried out. Lipids are a form of fatty acids derived from fruit extracts and thus are completely edible. The lipid extraction process is carried out using the procured machineries already included in the cost sheet. The lipid is further transformed in the form of powder that when made soluble can be applied on fruits. The farmers harvest the fruits from which it goes to the distributors who further sell it to big retail chains or to different markets. The distributors are already a customer of the fruit protecting alternatives and thus can be educated and partnered with to use product.

As the shelf life of fruits are getting increased, the fruit wastage will decrease.
Farmers generally harvest the fruits prematurely so that the ripening of the fruit happens during the selling and distribution process. Our product helps in avoiding the premature harvest of fruits and thus helps it in under-ripening as well as over-ripening.
Helps in decreasing the decaying of fruits that generally happens during exports as a result it can be exported to a longer distances
